Coordinate system

Before we do anything with Processing.js, I should talk about Coordinate systems. A Coordinate system is just how we describe position with numbers. If you were to tell me where your pencil is, you might say "it's 1 inch to the right of my paper". You probably wouldn't say exactly how far way your pencil is from your paper like that, but that's the basics of a coordinate system.
